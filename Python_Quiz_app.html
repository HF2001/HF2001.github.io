Written Response


Having assembled a quiz app, such a design created a couple issues. To begin, what problems did arise needed critical thinking. For the procedure, there was not a lot of space to practically use it, and to be honest, more difficulty came from figuring out where to place the procedure than actually implementing one. In addition, the procedure was implemented for use to call notifiers, and play sounds. Another issue that was apparent. Furthermore, whilst constructing the app, opportunities presented themselves for adding notifiers (concerning correct and incorrect answers). The notifiers were required elements in the development process and were implemented into the window of time in which a question is answered. The independent contributions to the app were the implementations of the questions and answers. In conclusion, the app is rather historical, and a good source of embarrassment...enjoy!
